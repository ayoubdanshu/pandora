What to Expect

Before Your Visit

Once you book, we'll send a confirmation email with all the details. We'll have your selected vehicle ready and waiting, with all paperwork prepared in advance.

During Your Test Drive

Our team will walk you through the vehicle's features and answer any questions. You'll have time to drive and experience the car in real-world conditions.

After Your Visit

No pressure to buy. Take your time to decide. If you're ready, our team will guide you through financing, warranties, and delivery options.

Test Drive Tips

  • Bring your license: A valid driver's license is required for all test drives
  • Plan ahead: Book your test drive in advance to ensure your preferred vehicle is available
  • Ask questions: Our team is here to help—ask about features, maintenance, or anything else
  • Take your time: There's no rush. Drive the vehicle and make sure it feels right for you
  • Bring a friend: A second opinion can be helpful when making such an important decision

Questions About Test Drives?

Our team is here to help. If you have questions about scheduling, what to bring, or what to expect, don't hesitate to reach out.
